# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## ParkSmart

### Smart Parking Management System

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Draft

**Author:** Bereket Amanuel Desta

**Project Type:** Full-Stack Portfolio Project

---

# 1. Product Vision

ParkSmart is a modern smart parking management platform designed to simplify the parking experience for drivers while providing parking providers with powerful digital tools to manage their parking facilities efficiently. The platform combines real-time parking availability, online reservations, intuitive dashboards, and secure authentication into a unified web and mobile solution.

The long-term vision is to create a scalable platform capable of supporting commercial parking businesses, corporate parking facilities, shopping malls, hospitals, universities, airports, and smart cities.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Urban drivers frequently spend valuable time searching for available parking spaces, leading to increased traffic congestion, fuel consumption, and frustration. Parking providers often depend on manual processes that limit operational efficiency and reduce visibility into parking utilization.

There is a need for a centralized platform that allows drivers to locate and reserve parking spaces while enabling parking providers to manage their operations digitally and efficiently.

---

The primary objectives of ParkSmart are:

* Reduce the time drivers spend searching for parking.
* Improve parking space utilization.
* Digitize parking management for providers.
* Provide real-time parking availability.
* Enable online reservations.
* Deliver an intuitive user experience.
* Build a scalable and maintainable software solution.

Organizations and individuals who own or manage parking facilities.

Businesses, hospitals, universities, shopping malls, and government institutions that require structured parking management.

Personnel responsible for managing and maintaining the platform.
